# Browser Terminals

> Free in-browser terminals for CLI practice at work — no local setup required

## GitHub Codespaces

- Go to **github.com/codespaces**
- Open your `lfcs-practice` codespace
- Press `Ctrl+`` ` to open the terminal
- Ubuntu-based — 60 free core-hours/month — auto-suspends after 30 min inactivity

## Google Cloud Shell

- Go to **shell.cloud.google.com**
- Log in with Google account — terminal loads immediately
- Debian-based — always free — 5GB persistent home directory
